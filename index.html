How To Play

  • To move left click left arrow key or 4
  • To move right click the right arrow key or 6
  • To hard drop click space or 8
  • To soft drop click the down arrow key or 2
  • To pause click Esc or F1 keys
  • To hold click Shift , C or 0
  • To rotate clockwise X and the up arrow key
  • To rotate counterclockwise cllick CTRL and Z
